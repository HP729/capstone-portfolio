Overall Capstone Experience

Choosing My Capstone Project

I chose to create the a adaptive task planner because I struggled with time management and wanted to develop a tool that would be genuinely useful to me. I also wanted a project that challenged me to learn new technologies and allowed me to build something functional from start to finish. This project gave me an opportunity to combine both learning and productivity into a single experience.

Being a full stack developer is something I want to do in the future. I wanted a challenge that required me to manage both frontend and backend development while implementing features from scratch. This project allowed me to practice designing architecture, working with databases, and integrating technologies that were new to me.

I know people who have simular struggles with time and task management. That gave me furthur motivation to create this application as there is a real use for it and I could discuss my work with people close to me and other students I meet thoughout my college career.

Approaching the Project

I approached the project by first creating a design specification document that listed what I wanted my app to be. I defined the technologies I plan to work with, features to implement, meetings to be done with experts, and defined what my stakeholders would want.

Then I took my design document and broke down my features into small pieces. I used these pieces to plan my work on a weekly basis. This planning phase was important as it ensured that I would stay on track.

As I made progress, I realized that things don’t go as planned. I had to learn to be adaptable. I had to adjust my plans as certain technologies were not the best or features needed to be changed. Meetings with experts, stakeholders, and my instructor all impacted my approach.

Learning Through Research

This experience pushed me to learn completely new technologies. Unlike a college course, I had to learn concepts on my own. Therefore, I relied on resources on the internet such as Youtube, documents from the technologies I used such as React. I learned React to build the frontend, Express and SQLite were used to create the backend, and Tailwind CSS was used for styling. Each technology required time for me to understand and use.

Research helped me make design changes as well. I’ve learned through multiple sources, which allowed me to understand and learn new ways of doing things. This allows my application to be more reliable and maintainable.

Motivation and Engagement

I’ve stayed motivated during the development of my project as I found it engaging to see something I create come to life. I enjoy the process of learning new technologies and being able to use them in action. Being given the opportunity to learn and create on your own schedule also gave me motivation as I get to decide how things are planned.

This project is connected to my future goal of becoming a full stack software engineer. By creating both the frontend and backend, I gain practical experience for the work I want to do in my future. Being ready for the workplace helped me stay engaged, learning on your own is something that I need to succeed.

Personal and Project Successes

To create a full stack application was a part of how I measured success throughout the process. I successfully created a working application that allows for creation, deletion, and editing of tasks. Breaking the project into smaller, manageable components and planning my weekly workload helped me stay successful.

Another success was my ability to learn and integrate new technologies. Most of the tools used for my application were new to me, however I was able to produce a product. I was able to overcome the knowledge I lacked at the beginning.

Maintaining communication with my stakeholders, experts, and instructor helped me ensure that my project met standards outside of what I imagined. The process of documenting and collecting feedback, and implementing those changes was a key source of success.

Next Steps

My next steps include continuing the development of my project until completion. I plan to implement a Pomodoro timer, adding the adaptive task suggestion feature, and integrating a calendar to make planning more interactive. With these features implemented, I will consider my project a success.

Reflecting on the experience, I have gained a lot of knowledge regarding both technical and personal growth. I learned the importance of planning your work, meeting with stakeholders and experts, and checking in with your manager. I learned how to communicate with technical concepts in written and verbal form.

Most importantly, I am better prepared for my future career. I've gained valuable hard and soft skills throughout the process. By continuing development, I can further gain experience and carry my experience into future projects.
