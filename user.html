Assisting College Students navigate their workload

As a student, you can use this application to plan tasks, track progress, and manage your workload in a way that adapts to changing deadlines and personal energy levels. Unlike a static to-do list, the application helps you stay organized by automatically adjusting your schedule as your workload and mood changes.

When creating tasks, you can set deadlines and monitor your progress over time. The system keeps track of each task and updates your workload as tasks are completed or deadlines approach. This allows you to quickly recognize what needs your attention so you can focus on the most important work first.

The application is designed to adapt to your productivity and workload. If tasks take longer than expected or new tasks are added, the system adjusts your schedule so that your workload stays balanced. This allows the application to help you stay organized and manage your responsibilities more effectively.
